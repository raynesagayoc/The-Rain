"Goyo: Ang Batang Heneral"

by rayne sagayoc

"Goyo" is a captivating portrayal of Gregorio del Pilar, a legendary Filipino hero, known for his bravery and patriotism during the Philippine-American War. In this artwork, the artist captures the essence of Goyo as a symbol of Filipino resilience and defiance against colonial oppression.

"Benny Krueger: Nightmare Weaver"

by rayne sagayoc

"Benny Kruger" is a surrealistic portrait capturing the enigmatic essence of a character who straddles the realms of dreams and nightmares. The artwork depicts Benny Kruger, a figure shrouded in mystery and intrigue, with piercing eyes that seem to pierce through the canvas, captivating viewers with their intensity.
